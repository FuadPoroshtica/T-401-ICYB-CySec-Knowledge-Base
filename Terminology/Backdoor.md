# Backdoor

**Term**: Hardware Backdoor

**Definition**: Hidden functionality at the hardware or firmware level that gives someone secret control or access, often impossible to remove with standard software tools.

**Context/Example**: A hardware backdoor (like the one that was in the Intel Management System) poses a risk even on a fully patched machine.
