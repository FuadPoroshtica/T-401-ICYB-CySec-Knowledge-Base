# Phishing Scams
**Term**: Phishing

**Definition**: A type of cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate entities to deceive individuals into providing sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details.

**Context/Example**: PhishTank has a database of Phishing links.

**Related Concepts**: Scams
