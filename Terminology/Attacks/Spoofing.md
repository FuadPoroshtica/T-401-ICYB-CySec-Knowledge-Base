# Spoofing

**Term**: Spoofing

**Definition**: Pretending to be someone or something else in order to gain trust or access.

**Context/Example**: Spoofed IP addresses or fake login pages.

**Related Concepts**: [Phishing](Phishing.md), impersonation, man-in-the-middle (MITM), identity theft.
