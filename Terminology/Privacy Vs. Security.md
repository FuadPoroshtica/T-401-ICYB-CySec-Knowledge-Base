# Privacy Vs. Security
**Privacy**: refers to an individuals control over their personal information. Involves setting boundaries on what data is shared, with whom, and for what purpose.

**Security**: Involves measures taken to protect data from unauthorized access and threats. This includes [[encryption]], [Firewalls](Defense%20&%20Control/Firewall.md), and [Access Control](Defense%20&%20Control/Access%20Control.md)
