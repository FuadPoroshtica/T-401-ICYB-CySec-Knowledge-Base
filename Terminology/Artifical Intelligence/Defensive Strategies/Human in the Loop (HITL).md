# Human in the Loop (HITL)
> [!definition] Human in the Loop (HITL)
> A defensive strategy for [LLMs](../LLM.md) where yo have human reviewers to validate AI outputs for high-risk tasks.
> Never allow an [LLM](../LLM.md) to execute high-consequence actions (Database deletion, money transfers) without human approval.
