# Cyber Defense

**Term**: Cyber Defense

**Definition**: Proactively preparing for, detecting, and responding to cyber attacks in order to limit or prevent damage.

**Context/Example**: Acting in anticipation to oppose an attack through cyber and cognitive domains.

**Related Concepts**: Incident response, intrusion detection, threat hunting
