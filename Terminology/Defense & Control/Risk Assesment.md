# Risk Assessment

**Definition**: To assess a certain risk of your computer system. Risk = Threat $\times$ Vulnerability $\times$ Cost.
- **Threat**: Who attacks?
- **Vulnerability**: Where are we weak?
- **Cost**: What do we lose?

**Context/Example**: A team of developers might have a meeting of what the worst case possible is, and they might come to a conclusion like “We shall not store credit card numbers”.
